The Initiative

Young Legal House is a dedicated initiative empowering law students across India.

We provide a common platform for learning, networking, and professional growth, connecting aspiring legal professionals with internships, training programs, competitions, events, and career opportunities. Our mission is to build a vibrant, informed, and collaborative legal community.
